## Human Element

### Figure work level

> Not every project needs people. But asking the question forces you to see the space as lived in, not just built. Select the level that fits this project.

- [ ] **Level 1 — Organic (no extra cost):** Builder/homeowner walks through during shoot. Candid moments only. Hands on materials, someone pausing at a view, figure in a doorway.
- [ ] **Level 2 — Directed (signature/editorial):** Brief one person to move naturally through key spaces. Plan 3-5 moment compositions during scout. Shoot during golden hour.
- [ ] **Level 3 — Produced (awards/portfolio heroes):** Book a model for 2 hours ($150-300). Art direct 5-8 compositions. These become portfolio differentiators and award submission heroes.
- [ ] **None — Pure documentation:** Architecture only, no human element needed.

### The key question

> Who lives in, works in, or moves through this space — and what's the single most compelling human moment that could happen here?

### Wardrobe notes

> If using a figure: neutral tones, no logos, no patterns. The person should complement the space, not compete with it. Dark clothing in light spaces, light clothing in dark spaces.
