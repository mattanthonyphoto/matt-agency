# Service Agreement Terms

Standard contract language for all engagements. Adapt per service type.

## Usage Rights & Licensing

- Client receives a **perpetual, non-exclusive license** for business use: website, social media, print marketing, proposals, award submissions, and publications
- Matt Anthony Photography **retains copyright** and the right to use images for portfolio, marketing, social media, case studies, and award entries
- Images may not be resold, sublicensed, or used for stock photography by either party
- **Credit requirement:** "Photo: Matt Anthony Photography" or "@mattanthonyphoto" when feasible (social media, publications, websites)
- Extended licensing (advertising, billboards, national campaigns) available at additional cost

## Payment Terms

| Milestone | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Booking deposit | 50% of total |
| Gallery delivery | Remaining 50% |

- Net 30 days from invoice date
- Accepted: e-transfer, credit card
- Late payment: 2% monthly interest after 30 days

## Cancellation & Rescheduling

| Scenario | Policy |
|----------|--------|
| Client cancels 7+ days out | Full deposit refund |
| Client cancels 3-6 days out | 50% deposit retained |
| Client cancels <3 days out | Full deposit retained |
| Weather cancellation | Reschedule at no cost (mutual agreement) |
| Photographer cancellation | Full refund or reschedule at client's choice |

## Delivery Timelines

| Service | Standard Turnaround |
|---------|-------------------|
| Project Photography | 15 business days |
| Award & Publication | 10 business days |
| Build & Team Content | 10 business days |
| Visual Partner Retainer | 10 business days per shoot |
| Rush delivery | 48 hours (add-on fee applies) |

## Revisions

- 2 rounds of minor edits included (crop, exposure, white balance)
- Additional edits or retouching beyond scope: $50/image
- Major reshoots billed as new session
- Scope changes require written change orders (per AOS)

## Liability

- Matt Anthony Photography carries professional liability insurance
- Not responsible for project delays outside photographer's control (weather, site access, staging)
- Force majeure clause for extraordinary circumstances

## Drone Operations

- All flights conducted under Transport Canada RPAS regulations
- Advanced Operations Certificate held
- Client responsible for confirming site access permissions for drone flight
- Weather-dependent — wind, rain, or low visibility may prevent aerial coverage
