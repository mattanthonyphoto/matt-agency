# Social Caption Templates

Fill-in-the-blank frameworks by post type and platform. Adapt the voice — professional but warm, no hype. For the full hooks library (50+ hooks), see `hooks-scripts-library.md`.

---

## Instagram Templates

### Project Reveal (Carousel)

**Hook options (pick one — this is the first line people see):**
- "Every angle in this project fights for your attention. Swipe."
- "The first time I walked into this space, I just stood there."
- "[Location] doesn't get enough credit for homes like this."
- "This is what happens when the builder actually cares about the details."
- "3 years of construction. 2 days to photograph. Here's the full story."
- "[Location]. [One-line description of the space.]"

**Body:**
[2-3 sentences about the project. Focus on what makes the DESIGN special — the architecture, the materials, the site, the collaboration. Not the photography. One specific detail that surprised you or stood out.]

**CTA options:**
- Full project on the site — link in bio.
- Swipe through to see the full story.
- [Tag client]: incredible work as always.

**Tags:** @[builder] @[architect] @[designer] @[trades if notable]

**Slide structure:** 8-10 images. Repeat first image as last slide (algorithm boost). Alternate wide/detail. 4:5 vertical (1080x1350px).

### Behind the Scenes (Reel or Story)

**Hook options:**
- "POV: You're photographing a $[X]M house and the sun disappears."
- "This is what 'waiting for the light' actually looks like."
- "The shot that almost didn't happen."
- "Every piece of furniture in this room was moved twice."
- "What the listing photo looks like vs. what the shoot looked like."

**Body:**
[1-2 sentences about what made this shoot interesting — the light, the access, the challenge, the team.]

**CTA:** More from this project coming soon.

**For Reels:**
- 0-3 sec: Hook as text overlay
- 3-15 sec: BTS footage showing the problem/setup
- 15-25 sec: The solution or result — final image reveal
- 25-30 sec: Text overlay CTA

### Educational / Tip Post (Carousel)

**Hook options:**
- "5 reasons your project photos aren't getting published."
- "The biggest mistake builders make with their marketing photography."
- "Stop staging your projects like a realtor. Here's the difference."
- "Why your phone photos are costing you clients."
- "[Number] things that separate good architectural photography from great."

**Slide structure:**
- Slide 1: Hook as bold text overlay on a compelling image
- Slides 2-6: One point per slide — image + short text overlay
- Slide 7: Summary or key takeaway
- Slide 8: "Want help with this? DM me or link in bio."

**Caption:**
[Restate the hook in 1 sentence. Add context. End with a question to drive comments.]

### Before/After (Carousel or Reel)

**Hook options:**
- "Construction → completion. Same angle, [X] months apart."
- "The raw file vs. the final delivery."
- "What I walked into vs. what the camera captured."

**Carousel structure:**
- Slide 1: "Before" with text overlay
- Slide 2: "After" — same angle, final image
- Slides 3-8: More before/after pairs or final hero shots
- Slide 9: Final image repeated

**Reel structure:**
- 0-3 sec: Construction/before footage with text "Before"
- 3-5 sec: Beat drop transition
- 5-15 sec: Slow reveal of final photography
- Caption: [1-2 sentences about the transformation]

## LinkedIn Templates

### Project Story (Document Carousel)

**Format:** Upload as PDF. 1080x1350px slides.

**Slide 1:** Hook text over hero image
**Slides 2-6:** Project images with minimal text — one per slide
**Slide 7:** Takeaway or insight

**Caption:**
```
[Hook — first 2 lines are all that show before "see more"]

[3-5 short paragraphs. One thought per line. Tell the project story from
a B2B angle — what was the builder's goal? What design challenge did they
solve? How does this project position them?]

[1-2 sentences connecting to a broader industry insight]

@[client company] @[architect] @[collaborators]

#architecturephotography #constructionmarketing #bcbuilder
```

**Link:** Put portfolio link in first comment, not in the post.

---

### Industry Insight (Text Post)

**Hook options:**
- "Builders spend $500K on a kitchen and $0 on photographing it."
- "I've seen the same photography mistake from 3 different firms this month."
- "The ROI of architectural photography isn't abstract. Here are the numbers."
- "If your portfolio looks like everyone else's, the camera isn't the problem."
- "Award submissions are won or lost before the photographer shows up."

**Structure:**
```
[Hook line 1]
[Hook line 2 — context or expansion]

[Problem statement: 2-3 sentences. Be specific — use a real example or
scenario your target client would recognize.]

[2-3 supporting points. Each a short paragraph. Practical and actionable.]

[Closing: Perspective shift, not a hard sell.]

#architecturephotography #designbuild #constructionmarketing
```

## Pinterest Templates

### Project Pin

**Title:** [Style] [Project Type] in [Location], BC | [Standout Feature]
Example: "West Coast Modern Custom Home in Whistler, BC | Floor-to-Ceiling Glazing"

**Description:**
```
[Project type] in [Location], British Columbia featuring [2-3 key design
elements]. Designed by [architect/designer], built by [builder].
[One sentence about the standout feature — materials, views, layout.]
[One sentence about what makes this project unique in the region.]
See the full project gallery at mattanthonyphoto.com.

#WestCoastModern #CustomHome #ArchitecturalPhotography #BCArchitecture
#[Location] #ResidentialDesign #[MaterialTag] #[StyleTag]
```

**Link:** Always point to the specific project page on your website.

**Alt text:** Describe the image with keywords. "Modern timber frame living room with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking Howe Sound in Squamish, BC."

## Caption Formatting Quick Reference

| Platform | Hook Position | Length | Hashtags | Links |
|----------|-------------|--------|----------|-------|
| Instagram Feed | First line, nothing before it | Under 300 words | 15-20, below caption | Link in bio |
| Instagram Reel | Text overlay in first 2 sec | 1-3 sentences | 15-20, below caption | Link in bio |
| Instagram Story | Text overlay | Minimal | None | Swipe-up/link sticker |
| LinkedIn | First 2 lines (pre-"see more") | 500-1500 characters | 3-5, in body | First comment only |
| Pinterest | In title field | 100-200 word description | 5-10, in description | Direct to project page |
