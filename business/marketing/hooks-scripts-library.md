# Hooks & Scripts Library

50+ proven hooks and fill-in-the-blank scripts organized by content pillar and platform. Every hook is designed to stop the scroll in the first 1-2 lines.

---

## How to Use This Library

1. Pick your **content pillar** (what you're posting about)
2. Pick your **platform** (where it's going)
3. Choose a **hook** that fits the tone
4. Fill in the **script template** beneath it
5. Adapt the voice — professional but warm, no hype

## Universal Hook Formulas

Use these as blueprints to generate hooks on the fly for any content:

### Curiosity Gap
- "The [$ amount] home that almost didn't get photographed..."
- "What you don't see in this photo took [X] hours to fix..."
- "The mistake that cost this builder their best [thing]..."

### Problem-Solution
- "If your [portfolio/marketing/submissions] aren't [converting/winning/landing], it's probably this..."
- "The real reason your [thing] doesn't [desired outcome]..."

### Pattern Interrupt
- "Forget everything you think about [topic]..."
- "Why I stopped [common practice]..."
- "The 'ugly' [thing] that became my best [outcome]..."

### Contrarian Take
- "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's what actually works..."
- "Everyone says [X]. I've found the opposite."
- "The unpopular opinion that changed my business..."

### Story Entry
- "Last week a [client type] asked me [surprising question]..."
- "I used to think [old belief] until [what changed]..."
- "3 years ago I [action]. Here's what happened..."

### Data/Proof
- "Homes with professional photography sell [X]% faster."
- "This [small change] [tripled/doubled] my [metric]."
- "[Specific number] projects later, here's what I know."

### Before/After
- "Construction → completion. Same angle, [X] months apart."
- "What I saw vs. what the camera captured."
- "The raw file vs. the final delivery."

## Platform-Specific Formatting Rules

### Instagram
- First line IS the hook — nothing before it
- Line break after the hook (use a period on its own line)
- Keep captions under 300 words for feed posts
- Hashtags: 15-20 per post, mix of tiers (see hashtag-system.md)
- Always tag collaborators in the image AND caption
- End carousels by repeating the first image (algorithm boost)

### LinkedIn
- First 2 lines must hook — that's all that shows before "see more"
- Use line breaks liberally (one thought per line)
- No links in the post body — put in first comment
- Tag people and companies (appears in their networks)
- Hashtags: 3-5 max, in the post body, not a block at the end
- Document/carousel posts (PDF upload) outperform images

### Pinterest
- Pin title: keyword-first, descriptive (not clever)
- Pin description: 100-200 words, natural keyword inclusion
- Always link back to the specific project page on your website
- Alt text matters — describe the image with keywords
- Board names should be search-friendly: "West Coast Modern Homes" not "Inspiration"

### Reels/TikTok
- Hook in first 2 seconds (text overlay + movement)
- 8-19 seconds for max completion rate, up to 60 for storytelling
- Raw > polished — don't over-produce
- Trending audio helps but isn't required
- Caption: short hook + context, hashtags
