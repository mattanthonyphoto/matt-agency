# Hashtag System

Tiered hashtag sets with rotation groups by content type and platform. Rotate sets to avoid algorithmic suppression.

---

## How to Use

- **Instagram:** 15-20 hashtags per post. Pick from each tier to build a balanced set.
- **LinkedIn:** 3-5 hashtags max, woven into the post or at the end.
- **Pinterest:** 5-10 keyword-rich hashtags in pin descriptions.
- **Rotate sets** — don't use the same exact group on consecutive posts.
- **Pre-built groups** at the bottom for quick copy-paste by post type.

## Maintenance

- **Monthly:** Check which hashtags are driving impressions (Instagram Insights → Content → individual post → Impressions from Hashtags). Drop underperformers, test new ones.
- **Quarterly:** Review for banned or shadowbanned hashtags. Instagram periodically restricts hashtags without notice.
- **Seasonal:** Add event-specific hashtags during award season (#georgieawards, #chba), construction season (#constructionprogress, #sitevisit), etc.
