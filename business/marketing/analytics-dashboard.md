## Platform Metrics

### Instagram

**Where to find:** Instagram Insights (Professional Dashboard → Insights) or Meta Business Suite.

| Metric | Where | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|-------|--------|---------------|
| Followers | Profile | Growing month-over-month | Audience size (vanity but directional) |
| Accounts reached (30 days) | Insights → Overview | 5,000+ | Content visibility |
| Accounts engaged (30 days) | Insights → Overview | 500+ | Active interest |
| Engagement rate per post | Insights → Content → Post | 5%+ | Content quality signal |
| Saves per post | Insights → Content → Post | 10+ | Strongest quality signal — people bookmark useful content |
| Shares/sends per post | Insights → Content → Post | 5+ | #1 algorithm signal for reaching new audiences |
| Reel plays (avg) | Insights → Content → Reels | 1,000+ | Top-of-funnel reach |
| Profile visits (weekly) | Insights → Overview | 50+ | Conversion intent |
| Website clicks (weekly) | Insights → Overview | 10+ | Actual traffic driven |
| Top-performing post | Insights → Content | Note it | Learn what works |
| Impressions from hashtags | Individual post insights | 10%+ of total | Hashtag strategy effectiveness |

## Revenue Attribution

Track these manually in a simple spreadsheet or CRM:

| Metric | How to Track | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| "How did you find me?" responses | Ask every new inquiry | Log platform |
| Inquiries citing Instagram | Note in CRM/spreadsheet | 1-2/quarter |
| Inquiries citing LinkedIn | Note in CRM/spreadsheet | 2-3/quarter |
| Inquiries from website (organic) | Note in CRM/spreadsheet | Track |
| Inquiries from referral (social-influenced) | Note in CRM/spreadsheet | Track |
| Revenue attributable to social | Sum of closed deals from social inquiries | Track quarterly |

**Simple attribution question to ask every new lead:**
"Thanks for reaching out — just curious, how did you come across my work?"

Log the answer. Over time, this tells you which platform is actually driving revenue, not just engagement.

## Benchmarks Reference

Industry benchmarks for architectural/photography accounts (2025-2026 data):

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| IG engagement rate | <2% | 2-4% | 5-7% | 8%+ |
| IG saves per post | <3 | 3-8 | 10-20 | 25+ |
| IG shares per post | <2 | 2-5 | 5-15 | 20+ |
| IG Reel plays | <200 | 200-500 | 1,000-5,000 | 10,000+ |
| LinkedIn impressions/post | <200 | 200-500 | 1,000-3,000 | 5,000+ |
| LinkedIn engagement rate | <1% | 1-2% | 3-5% | 6%+ |
| Pinterest monthly impressions | <1K | 1-10K | 10-50K | 100K+ |
| Pinterest outbound clicks/month | <10 | 10-50 | 50-200 | 500+ |

**Note:** These are for accounts of your size (500-5,000 followers). Engagement rates naturally decrease as follower count grows. A 5% engagement rate at 2,000 followers is the equivalent of 2-3% at 20,000.

## Quarterly Deep Review (add to monthly review every 3 months)

Every quarter, also assess:

1. **Platform ROI ranking:** Which platform is generating the most inquiries per hour invested?
2. **Content pillar performance:** Rank the 5 pillars by engagement. Shift the mix toward what's working.
3. **Follower quality audit:** Are you attracting target clients (builders, architects, designers) or just other photographers? Check follower profiles on LinkedIn, check IG audience demographics.
4. **Competitor check:** What are Ema Peter, Kyle Graham, Fyfe doing differently? Any formats or angles worth testing?
5. **Service alignment:** Is social content driving inquiries for the right services (retainers, project photography) or the wrong ones (one-off headshots, real estate)?
6. **Strategy adjustment:** Based on the quarter's data, what's the single biggest change to make next quarter?
