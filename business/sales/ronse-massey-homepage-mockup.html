Who We Are

A family-built approach to custom homes.

Joel and Xavier Massey are third-generation British Columbia homebuilders. What started as a shared passion for honest construction became Ronse Massey in 2007.

Every project starts with the same question: how should this home feel? From heritage restorations in Vancouver's established neighborhoods to marine-access builds on Keats Island, we bring the same commitment — craftsmanship you can see, capability you can trust, and honesty at every stage.

Heritage & Craft

Admirers of the Craftsman tradition.

Ronse Massey has a long-vested interest in the preservation of heritage homes and a deep appreciation for their cultural and aesthetic value. Restoring and renovating these historic projects is an art that demands passion, creativity, and distinctive problem-solving.

"Simple, pure design. Natural materials. Honest workmanship."

Joel Massey serves on the Vancouver Heritage Commission, advising City Council on heritage preservation and chairing the Heritage Awards jury. It's not just what we build — it's what we believe building should be.
