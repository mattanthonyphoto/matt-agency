# Architect Referral Flywheel — Sea-to-Sky & Greater Vancouver

*Research compiled March 26, 2026*

---

## The Strategy

One architect relationship → multiple builder relationships → flywheel effect.

```
ARCHITECT (1 relationship)
  → works with Builder A, B, C, D
  → You shoot 1 project for Architect
  → Cost-share introduces you to Builder A
  → Builder A hires you on next project (different architect)
  → That architect now knows you
  → Cycle repeats
```

**The math:** If one architect works with 4-5 builders/year, and each builder does 3-5 projects/year with various architects — one relationship could generate 20-30 annual project opportunities by Year 2.

## Pitching Architects vs. Builders

### What Architects Value That Builders Don't
- **Design intent** — does the photo communicate what they were trying to achieve?
- **Publication readiness** — will this get them into ArchDaily/Dezeen?
- **Spatial relationships** — light, material, proportion, sightlines
- **Conceptual storytelling** — narrative of building's relationship to site and context
- **Artistic vision** — a photographer who reinforces their design themes

### The Pitch to an Architect

**"I help you get published and win awards."**

1. "I understand your design intent. I photograph what you were trying to achieve."
2. "I deliver publication-ready images — 15+ images, 2880px minimum, editorially composed."
3. "I handle cost-sharing with the builder and trades. I bring the builder to the table."
4. "I know how to submit to Canadian Architect, Azure, Dezeen, and ArchDaily."
5. "Every project I shoot builds YOUR portfolio and gets YOUR name in front of more builders."

### Publications That Matter to Architects

**Tier 1 (International):** Dezeen (wants exclusivity), ArchDaily (15+ images at 2880px+), Architectural Digest, Dwell, Wallpaper*

**Tier 2 (Canada):** Canadian Architect (Photo Awards open August 2026), Azure Magazine, Western Living, NUVO Magazine

**Tier 3 (Industry):** Architizer (A+ Awards), HomeWorld Design, The Plan, Est Living

**Key fact:** Publications will NOT feature a project without professional photography. Full stop.

### Cost-Share Arrangements

Three typical models:
1. **Architect commissions, builder contributes** — Most common for publication-driven shoots
2. **Builder commissions, architect benefits** — For builder marketing
3. **Cost-share from start** — Photographer proposes to all parties upfront

**Economics example:** $6,000 shoot with 5 parties = ~$2,400/party (60% savings each), photographer earns $11,250+ total.
