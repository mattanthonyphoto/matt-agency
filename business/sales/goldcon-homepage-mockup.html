About

Built on Family,
Driven by Craft

Founded in 2013 by brothers Karim and Kaveh Goldan, Goldcon Construction has grown into one of North Vancouver's most respected custom home builders and renovation firms. With over 20 years of combined experience and a deep family legacy in the trades, we bring precision, integrity, and care to every project.

We specialize in custom homes, whole home renovations, kitchen and bathroom transformations, coach houses, and commercial builds across the North Shore and Greater Vancouver. Our approach is hands-on — we guide you through every stage from design consultation and budgeting to scheduling and final walkthrough.

Quality, communication, and attention to detail define how we work. Approximately 75% of our projects are fixed-price, so you know the final cost before we break ground. We build beautiful homes on time, on budget, and free of headaches.
