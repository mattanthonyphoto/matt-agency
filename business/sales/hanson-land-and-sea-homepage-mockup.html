1960s

A Sawmill on
Gambier Island

In the late 1960s, Lex Hanson left Southern California's development industry for something quieter. He built a hexagonal timber frame home on Gambier Island, set up a sawmill, and raised his family surrounded by heavy beams and raw coastal lumber.

That hex house became the family homestead — and the blueprint for everything that followed.

1990s

Marine Roots,
Coastal Craft

Marlin and Ryan grew up building alongside their father — piers, docks, and waterfront structures that demanded durability against the Pacific. The marine DNA stuck: massive beams, simple-yet-strong joinery, and a respect for what the coast can throw at a structure.

The houses we make reflect where they're from.

Marlin Hanson
2010s

From Hex Studio
to Dwell Magazine

A 250-square-foot hexagonal studio for a birth doula. Hand-split western red cedar shakes, Douglas fir hex flooring, clay plaster walls. Dwell came calling. Then the Roberts Creek Family Home with September Architecture. Then the Airstream Ryan found rusting on a job site and restored from nothing.

Four Dwell features in a decade. Not because they chase press — because the work speaks.

Now

Two Brothers,
Two Regions

Today Marlin runs the Sunshine Coast operation from Roberts Creek to Egmont. Ryan leads Powell River and the surrounding islands. Between them, 16 named projects, architect partnerships with Battersby Howat and September Architecture, and a crew that builds like the coast taught them — durable, honest, and rooted in the landscape.

What We Build

Five Disciplines,One Standard.

From a 250-square-foot hexagonal studio to a multi-million-dollar oceanfront home — the same commitment to craft and native materials.

01

Custom Homes

Design-build homes shaped by their site. From remote island retreats to architect-designed oceanfront residences, built from local timber with lasting craftsmanship.

02

General Contracting

Full-service project management with transparent budgets, regular review meetings, and skilled trades coordination that keeps complex builds on track.

03

Renovations

Kitchen and bathroom makeovers, additions, extensions, and full home transformations that respect the existing structure while reimagining how you live.

04

Commercial

New builds, tenant improvements, and developments. The same quality standards and personal approach we bring to residential, scaled for commercial.

05

Outdoor Structures

Saunas, studios, bunkhouses, and decks built for the coast. The same craftsmanship as our custom homes, designed for outdoor living year-round. Ask us about the Hex Studio — 250 square feet of hand-split cedar and Douglas fir that landed in Dwell Magazine.
