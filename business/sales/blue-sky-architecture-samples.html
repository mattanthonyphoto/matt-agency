A Note for Bo

Thank you for looking

Bo, thank you for taking the time. What drew me to Blue Sky's work in the first place was that relationship between building and landscape, architecture that reads as part of its site rather than placed on top of it. That sensibility runs through everything I try to do with a camera.

I photograph completed architecture, and I also produce short documentary videos of the build process. The goal in both cases is the same: give editors, juries, and peers direct access to the design intent behind the work.

Below is a cross-section of recent projects across BC, from coastal to alpine. I've organized them the way they appear on my site, so you can see the range and the consistency.
