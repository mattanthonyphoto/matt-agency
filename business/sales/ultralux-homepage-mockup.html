About Us

Over 20 Years of Building Excellence

Ultralux Homes Inc designs and builds boutique single-family homes with over 20 years of experience. There is nothing quite like living in a home which you have helped design, and tailored to your family's every need.

We are a design/build firm, providing services from architectural plans through permitting to construction. Our multidisciplinary team includes experts in design, onsite construction, and project management.

Building a new home can be one of the most stressful ventures you undertake. We exist to take that stress away, guiding you through every step with transparency and communication.

What We Do

Our Services

01

Custom Homes

A custom home is a one of a kind design created to meet the specific needs of you and your family. We specialize in boutique single-family homes in Vancouver and Burnaby.

02

Duplex Homes

Convert a single property into two legal properties. Occupy one unit while selling or renting the other — a smart way to maximize property value.

03

Laneway Homes

Secondary detached structures built on existing lots. Smarter living in smaller spaces — a sustainable housing option gaining popularity across Vancouver.

04

Major Renovations

From structural upgrades to complete interior transformations. Our team manages every detail of your renovation with the same standards as our new builds.

Our Process

Consult. Design. Interior. Build.

01

Consult

We connect with you to understand your needs, budget, and quality expectations. Site assessments, code review, and high-level budget planning.

02

Design

Architects and designers collaborate with you to visualize the project. Permits filed, engineers engaged, and detailed budget estimates developed.

03

Interior

Select how your interior space reflects your vision. Fixtures, finishings, and full budget transparency on how design decisions impact costs.

04

Build

Construction begins with a set schedule. Our team and trusted subcontractors execute while you receive transparent timeline and budget tracking.
