About Kombi Construction

Kombi Construction is a family-owned general contracting company with over 30 years of experience building and renovating homes across the North Shore. With deep roots in North and West Vancouver, we specialize in custom homes, renovations, complex deck builds, structural concrete, and underpinning.

Founded in 1994, we bring precision craftsmanship and hands-on project management to every build. From structural concrete and underpinning to full custom homes, our team handles the complexity so you can focus on the vision.
