Technical Foundation

The engine under the hood needs work.

Your site runs WordPress with the Avada theme on a Cloudflare CDN. Rank Math handles SEO. The fundamentals are in place, but the implementation has serious gaps that are throttling your search visibility.

Platform Stack

  • WordPress + Avada Theme (Fusion Builder)
  • Rank Math SEO plugin
  • Cloudflare CDN
  • WP Rocket caching (installed but underperforming)
  • WordPress Multisite (main + Vancouver + Philadelphia)

Key Metrics

  • Homepage HTML: 677 KB (bloated)
  • Inline CSS: 302 KB across 12 blocks
  • External JS files: 70 scripts
  • Images: 46 on homepage, only 1 lazy-loaded
  • Crawl-delay: 10s (severely throttling bots)

Double Redirect Chain

Your secondary domain colourcraft.ca currently redirects through a double 301: colourcraft.ca → http://colourcraftpainting.com → https://colourcraftpainting.com. Every extra redirect wastes crawl budget and adds latency. This should be a single redirect straight to the HTTPS primary domain.

Multisite Content Fragmentation

Service pages exist on both the main site (/interior-house-painting/) and the Vancouver subsite (/vancouver/interior-house-painting-services/). Two pages targeting the same keywords creates keyword cannibalization, where your own pages compete against each other in search results instead of consolidating authority.

Priority Issues

What needs to be fixed, and when.

25 issues identified across four priority levels. The P0 items are actively hurting your credibility right now. P1 items are costing you search visibility every day they remain unfixed.

P0 — Fix Immediately

Critical

Placeholder contact info in production header

Your site header shows "1-800-555-5555" and "info@example.com" as contact information. Visitors are seeing fake placeholder data. Your real phone (778-744-0192) only appears lower on the page.

Critical

Schema markup contains "COMPANYPHONE" placeholder

Your structured data sends the literal string "COMPANYPHONE" to Google instead of your actual phone number. This corrupts your search appearance and local SEO signals.

Critical

Broken JavaScript rendering as text on /book/ page

The H1 tag on your booking page displays raw code: ' + (loc.headline || loc.name) + '. Visitors see broken template syntax instead of a headline.

Critical

Google Reviews page returns 404

The /google-reviews URL returns a 404 error. Any links pointing to this page from emails, social, or print materials are sending visitors to a dead end.

P1 — Fix This Week

High

Multiple H1 tags on key pages

Homepage has 2 H1 tags, Squamish page has 3. Google expects one H1 per page. Multiple H1s dilute your keyword targeting and confuse crawlers about what each page is about.

High

Missing H1 tags on Colour Consultation and Contact pages

Two important pages have no H1 tag at all. Without an H1, search engines have no primary signal for what the page is about.

High

No LocalBusiness schema anywhere on the site

For a local painting company, LocalBusiness schema is essential. It tells Google your address, hours, service area, and phone number in a structured format. You are missing this entirely.

High

No FAQPage schema despite FAQ sections on 5+ pages

You have FAQ content on multiple service pages but no FAQPage schema markup. Adding schema would qualify you for rich FAQ snippets in Google, dramatically increasing your search result real estate.

High

Double 301 redirect chain on colourcraft.ca

The secondary domain passes through two redirects before reaching your site. Should be a single redirect to https://colourcraftpainting.com.

High

Crawl-delay: 10 in robots.txt

You are telling search engine bots to wait 10 seconds between requests. This severely throttles how quickly Google can index your pages. Remove it or reduce to 1-2 seconds.

P2 — Fix This Month

Medium

No geographic keywords in service page titles

Every service page title reads "Service Name | Colour Craft" without any location context. Adding "Vancouver", "BC", or "Sea to Sky" would immediately improve local relevance.

Medium

Referral program meta description is 350+ characters

Google truncates at ~155-160 characters. Your referral page meta description is more than double that, so Google is cutting it off mid-sentence.

Medium

Keyword cannibalization between main site and Vancouver subsite

Duplicate service pages across the main site and Vancouver subsite compete against each other for the same keywords, splitting your authority.

Medium

Only 6 blog posts total

Content marketing is nearly nonexistent. A painting company in multiple markets should be publishing at least 2 posts per month targeting local painting queries.

Medium

Homepage weighs 677 KB with 70 external scripts

Despite having WP Rocket installed, the Avada theme generates massive inline CSS. 302 KB of inline CSS and 70 JavaScript files result in slow load times.

Medium

Only 1 of 46 images has lazy loading

Without lazy loading, all 46 homepage images load at once, killing page speed on mobile. Modern best practice is to lazy-load everything below the fold.

Medium

Sitemap covers only 28 pages, misses Vancouver subsite

Your 19+ Vancouver subsite pages are invisible to Google's sitemap. No post-sitemap exists for blog content either.

Schema & Content

What Google sees vs. what you built.

Schema markup is structured data that tells search engines exactly what your business is, what you offer, and where you operate. Your site has basic schema from Rank Math, but is missing the types that matter most for a local service business.

What You Have

  • Organization schema (basic)
  • WebSite with SearchAction
  • WebPage with dates
  • VideoObject (YouTube embed)
  • Canonical URLs (correct)

What You're Missing

  • LocalBusiness (address, hours, service area)
  • FAQPage (FAQ content exists, no markup)
  • Service (painting service types)
  • AggregateRating / Review
  • BreadcrumbList
  • HowTo (process page)

Schema Bug

Your structured data currently sends "telephone": "COMPANYPHONE" to Google. This is a template placeholder that was never replaced with your actual phone number. It also uses a dual @type: ["Organization", "Person"] which is unusual and may confuse search engines.

Blog Content

Your entire blog consists of 6 posts, all under the Vancouver subsite and not accessible from main navigation. For context, a painting company targeting multiple markets should aim for 2-4 posts per month covering topics like seasonal painting tips, project spotlights, colour trends, and location-specific guides.
