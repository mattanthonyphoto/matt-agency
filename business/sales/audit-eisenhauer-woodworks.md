## 1. SEO — Technical Audit

### The Yoast Paradox
Eisenhauer has Yoast SEO installed — the industry-standard WordPress SEO plugin. But it appears to have never been configured. Every field that Yoast provides (meta descriptions, schema, Open Graph, focus keywords) is empty or broken.

### Meta Descriptions: 0 out of 35+ pages
Not a single page on the entire site has a meta description. Google auto-generates snippets from page content, which means every search result is an uncontrolled first impression.

### Title Tags: Multiple Are Garbled / Nonsensical
| Page | Title Tag | Problem |
|---|---|---|
| Inquire | "Eisenhauer Woodworks Inquire \| Inquire Homework's & Woodworks" | "Homework's" — garbled, possibly AI-generated |
| Larch Drive | "Eisenhauer woodworks Larch Drive \| Dr Larch \| Larch Drive" | "Dr Larch" — nonsensical |
| McKinney-Behn | "Eisenhauer McKinney Behn Residence \| Bc Woodworks Contact" | "Bc Woodworks Contact" — garbled |
| Taqueria | "Eisenhauer Woodworks Taqueria \| Taqueria el Corazon Bulid Now" | **"Bulid"** — typo visible in Google SERPs |
| Build Guide | "Eisenhauer Woodworks About \| Eisenhauer Luxury construction" | **Duplicate** of About page title |
| Thank You | "Eisenhauer Woodworks Inquire \| Inquire Homework's & Woodworks" | **Duplicate** of Inquire page title |
| Demo Shop | "Eisenhauer Woodworks Demo Shop \| Demo Shop Woodworks" | **Test page still live and indexed** |

### Structured Data / Schema: Zero
- No LocalBusiness schema
- No Organization schema
- No Article schema on blog posts
- No FAQ schema on the FAQ page (which has 6 structured Q&As — a direct rich snippet opportunity lost)
- No BreadcrumbList, no Review, no CreativeWork schema on any page

### Image Alt Text: Systematic Failure
- **Coulter Residence (28 images): 0/28 have alt text**
- **Morrison Farmhouse (25 images): 0/25 have alt text**
- Their two flagship projects — 53 images with zero accessibility or SEO value
- Larch Drive: same generic alt text copy-pasted across 6+ images
- Estimated 60–80+ images sitewide missing alt text

### Indexation: Only 5 Pages Indexed
A `site:eisenhauer.build` search returns only **5 indexed pages** out of 35+ in their sitemap. The services page, about page, FAQ, build guide, and 4+ portfolio pages are NOT indexed by Google.

For comparison: `site:munter.ca` returns 10+ indexed pages.

### Additional Technical Failures
- **robots.txt has `crawl-delay: 60`** — tells Bing to wait 60 seconds between requests (dramatically slows crawling)
- **No sitemap URL declared in robots.txt**
- **No Google Analytics or Tag Manager detected** — zero visibility into traffic or user behavior
- **No conversion tracking on thank-you page** — cannot measure form submissions
- **No Open Graph tags** — social sharing shows unpredictable previews
- **No WebP images** — still serving all JPEG/PNG
- **No lazy loading detected**
- **Legacy Squarespace site still live** at jeisenhauerwoodworks.squarespace.com — creating duplicate content
- **Multiple H1 tags** on Projects, FAQs, and Build Guide pages

## 4. Photography & Visual Assets

### What Exists
- **Coulter Residence:** 28 photos — professional quality, strong compositions
- **Morrison Sub Farmhouse:** 25 photos — professional quality, but Flickr-sourced (filenames contain Flickr IDs like `53075783185_9c807e0c06_k.jpeg` — the "_k" suffix is Flickr's "Large 2048" size, not full-resolution originals)

- **Woodworking portfolio:** 16 detail images
- **Commercial:** Taqueria (5), Angry Hen Brewing (16)

### What's Missing

**Zero twilight/dusk photography.** For $800K–$1.8M mountain homes with views, twilight exteriors are the gold standard of luxury marketing. Not one image on the site.

**Zero aerial/drone photography.** Mountain context, valley settings, property scale — none of this is captured.

**Zero process/craftsmanship photography.** Jeremy's entire brand is "built by hand," Wabi-Sabi, 4-generation woodworking heritage, Inside Passage fine woodworking training. Yet there is not one image on the site showing:
- Hands at work
- Timber selection
- Joinery details
- Crew collaboration
- Site conditions
- Material sourcing

This is the single biggest brand contradiction on the site. The story is about craft. The visuals show none of it.

**Zero lifestyle photography.** Every home feels empty and unlived-in. No people, no warmth, no "what it feels like to live here."

**Zero before/after documentation.** No renovation transformations captured.

**Only 6 custom home projects shown** after "nearly 20 years" of building. Either the work isn't being photographed, or it isn't being shown. Both are problems.

### The Flickr Pipeline Problem
The Morrison Farmhouse images use Flickr export filenames. This is not a professional photography delivery pipeline — it suggests photos were taken casually or by a semi-pro and uploaded to Flickr before being pulled to the website at reduced resolution. A professional architectural photographer delivers full-resolution files via cloud gallery with usage rights, not Flickr.

### Rachel Pick Photography
One photographer — Rachel Pick (rachelpick.ca) — is credited for workshop/woodworking documentation from the Kaslo era. No architectural photographer appears to be associated with the current Kimberley projects.
