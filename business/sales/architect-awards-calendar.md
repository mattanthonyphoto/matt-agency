## Awards Calendar

### AFBC Architectural Awards of Excellence (the "LGs")
- **What:** Highest provincial architecture award in BC. Includes Lieutenant Governor Awards.
- **Categories:** LG Awards, Design Excellence, Bing Thom Legacy Award, Emerging Firm, Innovation, Unbuilt, Equity, Design Distinction, Special Jury
- **Eligible:** Projects in BC/Yukon completed in prior ~3 years
- **Cycle:** Biennial (odd years)
- **Deadline:** ~July 25
- **Photography:** Professional photography of completed projects required
- **Photographer credited:** Yes

### RAIC Governor General's Medals in Architecture
- **What:** Canada's most prestigious architecture award. Up to 12 medals per cycle.
- **Eligible:** Canadian architects, projects in Canada or internationally
- **Cycle:** Biennial (even years — 2026 is live)
- **Deadline:** December 11, 2026 (10pm ET)
- **Fee:** ~$415 CDN (BC rate, GST included)
- **Photography:** Professional photography required. Since 2024, must include Energy Use Intensity and Thermal Energy Demand Intensity metrics.
- **Photographer credited:** Yes

### RAIC Annual Awards
- **What:** Gold Medal, Emerging Practice, Advocate for Architecture, Journalism & Media, Research & Innovation, Prix du XXe Siècle
- **Deadline:** January 9 (10pm ET) — for the following year's conference
- **Fee:** ~$415 CDN
- **Photographer credited:** Yes

### Canadian Architect Awards of Excellence
- **What:** Run by Canadian Architect magazine. Focused on **unbuilt/future projects.**
- **Opens:** ~July-August
- **Deadline:** ~September 12
- **Winners:** Published in December issue
- **Photography:** Renderings for unbuilt — less relevant for Matt directly

### Canadian Architect Photo Awards of Excellence
- **What:** Photography-specific award — 7th year running
- **Who can enter:** Architectural photographers (Matt can enter directly)
- **Requirements:** Best image of a Canadian building, no more than 2 years old
- **Opens:** ~August 1
- **Deadline:** ~September 12
- **Fee:** ~$75 CDN
- **Winners:** Published in December issue and all digital platforms
- **Photographer credited:** Yes — featured prominently
- **Why it matters:** Winning or being featured = credibility with every architect in Canada

### Wood Design & Building Awards
- **What:** National program by Canadian Wood Council celebrating wood architecture
- **Opens:** ~April
- **Deadline:** Early bird May 31, Final June 27
- **Categories:** Honor, Merit, Citation
- **Fee:** **Free for BC projects** (courtesy of WoodWorks BC)
- **Also entered into:** WoodWorks BC regional awards automatically
- **Photography:** Professional photography required
- **Photographer credited:** Yes

### Architizer A+ Awards
- **What:** Major international award with global exposure
- **Opens:** ~October-November
- **Deadline:** ~February (multiple deadlines with escalating fees)
- **Categories:** Jury + Popular Choice winners
- **Timeline:** Finalists May, Winners June
- **Photography:** 1-12 images per submission
- **Photographer credited:** Yes, on Architizer platform
