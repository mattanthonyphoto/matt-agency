Service 01

Project Photography

Complete visual documentation of your finished build, composed to honour the design intent and craftsmanship behind the work.

Your project deserves imagery as considered as the design itself

You've spent months, sometimes years, getting every detail right. The material choices, the way light enters a room, the relationship between the structure and its site. A project shoot captures all of that in a single, cohesive visual story.

Every shoot begins with understanding what you built and why. I review plans, walk the site, and talk through your design intent before composing a single frame. The result is photography and short form film that communicates your decisions with the clarity they deserve.

Architecture, interiors, detail vignettes, aerial, and twilight coverage are all part of a standard engagement. No add ons, no line items. You get a complete visual record of the project, ready for your website, proposals, social media, and client presentations.
