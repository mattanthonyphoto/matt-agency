The Process

A typical session includes a pre shoot consultation to understand your project and goals, a full day or half day on site capturing exterior, interior, detail, and aerial photography, professional post production editing, and delivery of high resolution images through a curated online gallery within 5 to 10 business days.

Before every shoot, I review architectural plans, renderings, or previous photos to understand orientation, light conditions, and composition opportunities. I'll send you a prep checklist covering staging tips, timing, and key design details to highlight. This ensures we make the most of our time on site.

Most residential architectural shoots take between 3 and 6 hours depending on the size of the property and the scope of coverage. Larger properties, multi building sites, or projects that include twilight photography may require a full day or a return visit.

Drone & Technical

Yes. I'm a Transport Canada certified Advanced Drone Pilot. Aerial photography is part of how I work and is included in most architectural shoots. It adds context, reveals a project's relationship to its surroundings, and gives clients a perspective that ground level photography can't achieve on its own.

Yes. Drone flights are regulated by Transport Canada and certain areas have airspace restrictions, including near airports, helipads, and some national parks. I handle all flight planning and regulatory compliance. If a location has restrictions, I'll let you know during the planning stage and suggest alternative approaches if needed.

Yes. I produce cinematic video content that showcases the flow, scale, and atmosphere of a property. This includes ground level walkthroughs, aerial flyovers, and detail sequences. Video is crafted for web, social media, and presentation use.

I shoot with professional full frame mirrorless cameras and a range of architectural lenses including tilt shift glass for perspective correction. For aerial work, I use a commercial grade drone platform. All post production is done in Lightroom and Photoshop with a focus on natural, editorial quality results.

Deliverables & Licensing

Final images are typically delivered within 5 to 10 business days through a curated online gallery. You'll receive both high resolution files for print and web optimized versions. Rush delivery is available if you're working toward a deadline.

You'll receive high resolution JPEGs optimized for both print and digital use. Web sized versions and social media crops are available on request. If you need TIFF files or specific formats for a publication, I can accommodate that as well.

Pricing & Packages

Pricing is based on the scope of each project including the number of images, time on site, location, and any additional services like drone photography or videography. I provide custom quotes after an initial conversation about your project.

Yes. For builders, architects, and firms with multiple projects throughout the year, I offer retainer partnerships that include a set number of shoots at a preferred rate. This ensures consistent visual quality across your portfolio and gives you priority scheduling.

A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking, with the balance due upon delivery of the final images. I accept e transfer, credit card, and bank transfer.

Aerial photography is included in most architectural packages. For standalone drone sessions or projects that require extended aerial coverage, it's quoted separately. I'll always be upfront about costs during the proposal stage.
