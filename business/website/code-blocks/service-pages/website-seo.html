The Problem

Your best work is invisible if no one can find it

Your website is your most important salesperson. It works around the clock, it is the first thing prospects check, and it shapes their impression before you ever speak. If it is not performing, you are leaving projects on the table.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about website and SEO

Why Squarespace instead of WordPress or a custom build?

Squarespace gives us the best balance of design flexibility, speed, and maintainability for portfolio-driven businesses. It handles hosting, security, and SSL out of the box, so you never worry about plugin updates or server maintenance. With custom code blocks, we achieve the same visual quality as a fully custom site at a fraction of the ongoing cost.

How long does a full website build take?

Most builds take 4 to 8 weeks from kickoff to launch. The timeline depends on the number of pages, how quickly we receive content and feedback, and whether photography is being delivered alongside the build. We set clear milestones so you always know where things stand.

What is GEO and why does it matter?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It is the practice of optimizing your website so that AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity reference your business in their responses. As more people use AI to find service providers, GEO ensures your firm shows up in those conversations alongside traditional search results.

Do I need a growth retainer after the site launches?

A website launch is the starting line. Without ongoing SEO, content updates, and technical maintenance, your rankings plateau and your site becomes stale. The growth retainer keeps your site active, your content fresh, and your search visibility improving month over month. That said, it is not required. The site we build will be strong on its own.

Can I bundle this with photography or social media?

Yes. Many of our clients pair website and SEO with our Creative Partner retainer, which covers photography, video, social media, and web under one monthly agreement. It is the most efficient way to keep every channel consistent without managing multiple vendors.
