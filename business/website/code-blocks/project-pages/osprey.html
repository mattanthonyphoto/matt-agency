Warmth, craftsmanship, and timeless design in a thoughtful interior renovation

Osprey, a Pitt Meadows interior renovation by Koze Design & Build, beautifully blends warmth, craftsmanship, and timeless design. The home showcases thoughtful details such as custom millwork, arched passageways, and natural wood cabinetry that bring both character and functionality to each space.

The kitchen highlights a balance of elegance and practicality with brass fixtures, built-in storage, and a grand island perfect for gathering. Sunlit living spaces, curated shelving, and spa-like bathrooms emphasize comfort while maintaining a refined aesthetic. Through careful attention to materials and natural light, this renovation reflects a seamless blend of modern living and classic design sensibilities.
