Honouring Whistler's heritage, keeping the soul of the log home alive with a modern level of refinement

This project was all about honouring Whistler's heritage, keeping the soul of the log home alive while bringing a modern level of refinement. The goal was not to hide the logs, but to celebrate them, to let the craftsmanship shine through proportion, lighting, and contrast. Raw timber was balanced with natural stone, oak, and soft fabrics, creating a timeless palette that feels connected to nature yet distinctly elevated. Each space was designed as its own experience, with unique shapes, textures, and moments of calm. It is a home that honours its roots while fully embracing modern living.
