Designed to celebrate Mount Currie and the surrounding forest, this 4,800 sq ft home frames nature at every turn

Designed by Stark Architecture, built by Balmoral Construction, and with interiors by Britt Lothrop, this 4,800 sq ft Pemberton home is a study in how architecture can honour its setting. Expansive glazing by Innotech captures Mount Currie and the surrounding forest from nearly every room.

Inside, white oak millwork by Blueprint Millwork, a stone kitchen island, and warm material palettes create spaces that feel both refined and grounded. From the dramatic staircase to the twilight exterior, the home balances bold architectural form with the natural beauty of the Sea-to-Sky corridor.
