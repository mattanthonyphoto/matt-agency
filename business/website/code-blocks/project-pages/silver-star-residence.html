Perched high above the treetops at Silver Star Mountain Resort, this striking modern retreat is more than a home

Designed by Ian Paine Construction and Design and built by Rich Built Homes, the residence balances drama and restraint with intention. Dark-stained timber, raw stone, and metal detailing ground the structure in its snowy context, while expansive windows invite the sweeping landscape into the core of the home.

Inside, rugged materials meet refined aesthetics. A monolithic stone kitchen wall becomes a sculptural centerpiece, while layered lighting and soft natural textures bring warmth to the home's bold geometry. From the panoramic living room views to the cedar-wrapped lounge, every space is crafted to enhance atmosphere, light, and livability.
