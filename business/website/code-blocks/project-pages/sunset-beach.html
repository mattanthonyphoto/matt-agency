A masterclass in light, space, and refined coastal minimalism

This luxury penthouse in Vancouver is a masterclass in light, space, and refined coastal minimalism. Perched high above the skyline, the residence offers panoramic views of the city and False Creek, framed by floor-to-ceiling glazing that floods every room with natural light.

The interiors are styled in soft neutrals, layered textures, and sculptural accents, creating a serene yet elevated atmosphere. From the spa-like bathrooms to the light-filled bedrooms and open-concept living space, every detail was photographed to highlight clean lines, material depth, and spatial clarity.
