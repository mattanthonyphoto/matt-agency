West Coast contemporary design defined by clean lines, natural materials, and a seamless connection to landscape

Set on a rugged shoreline along the Sunshine Coast, this modern home by Summerhill Fine Homes exemplifies West Coast contemporary design at its finest. Floor to ceiling glass frames panoramic ocean views, while dark wood cladding blends the structure into its forested surroundings.

Inside, minimalist interiors feature polished concrete floors, a sculptural suspended fireplace, and a soft, neutral palette that enhances the home's tranquil atmosphere. Designed for effortless living and a deep connection to nature, this home is a testament to refined craftsmanship and coastal modern architecture.
