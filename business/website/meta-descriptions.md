## Implementation Notes

### Character Count Targets
- **Meta descriptions:** 150-160 characters (Google truncates at ~160)
- **Title tags:** Under 60 characters where possible (Google truncates at ~60)
- **OG descriptions:** No hard limit, but 200-300 characters is the sweet spot for social previews

### Formatting Rules
- No quotation marks in meta descriptions (truncated in SERPs)
- Pipe `|` separator in title tags
- Primary keyword placed near the beginning of each description
- Geographic terms included where relevant
- Soft CTAs or value propositions where natural — no pushy language

### Title Tags Needing Attention
- `/creative-partner` — new page, suggested title: **Ongoing Photography Partner for Builders & Designers | Matt Anthony**
- Several project page titles exceed 60 characters — monitor SERP appearance and shorten if truncation is an issue
- Location page titles are concise and keyword-forward — no changes needed

### Priority Implementation Order
1. **Core pages and service pages** — highest traffic, most impact
2. **Location pages** — key for local SEO and geographic targeting
3. **Case studies** — support conversion from service page visitors
4. **Journal posts** — long-tail search and topical authority
5. **Project pages** — portfolio browsing, lower direct search volume
