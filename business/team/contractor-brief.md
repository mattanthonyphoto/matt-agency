# Contractor / Second Shooter Brief

For anyone joining a Matt Anthony Photography shoot.

## About the Brand

Matt Anthony Photography is an architectural and interiors photography business. We document completed projects, construction in progress, and team content for architects, builders, and designers across BC. The work is natural, precise, and intentional — not flashy or stylized.

Read `style-guide.md` in this folder before your first shoot.

## Your Role On Set

- **Second shooter:** Capture supplementary angles, details, and behind-the-scenes while Matt handles hero compositions
- **Assistant:** Manage lighting, move staging elements, hold reflectors, handle drone prep
- Role will be confirmed before each shoot

## On-Set Standards

- **Arrive 15 minutes early.** No exceptions.
- **Dress:** Clean, dark, neutral clothing. Closed-toe shoes. No logos or bright colors.
- **Client interaction:** Friendly and professional. Let Matt lead all client communication unless asked otherwise. Don't make promises about deliverables or timelines.
- **Phone:** Silent. No personal phone use on set.
- **Move quietly** through the space — we're often shooting in occupied or staged homes.

## Equipment

### You Bring
- Camera body (full frame preferred)
- Wide angle lens (16-35mm range)
- Standard zoom (24-70mm range)
- Tripod
- Spare batteries + cards
- Laptop for on-site backup (if requested)

### Matt Provides
- Lighting equipment
- Drone (only Matt operates — TC certification required)
- Shot list and creative direction
- Any specialty gear needed

## Image Handling

- **Memory cards stay with Matt** at end of shoot
- All raw files are property of Matt Anthony Photography
- Editing is handled by Matt unless explicitly delegated
- If you're assigned editing, follow `style-guide.md` exactly
- Do not post, share, or use any images from the shoot without written permission

- All images produced during the shoot are owned by Matt Anthony Photography

## Confidentiality

- Client details, locations, and project information are confidential
- Do not share addresses, client names, or project details with anyone outside the team
- Some projects may be under NDA — Matt will flag these in advance
