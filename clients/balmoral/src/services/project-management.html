Project Management

Expert construction project management from pre-construction to closeout across Whistler, Pemberton, and Squamish.

Professional oversight for every phase of your build

Not every project requires a full general contractor. Some homeowners have their own trades lined up. Some have an architect driving the design but need someone on the ground coordinating the build. Others are mid-project and realize they need professional oversight to keep things on track. That's where Balmoral's standalone project management service comes in.

We offer dedicated construction project management as its own service because we've seen firsthand what happens when builds lack proper coordination. Budgets drift. Schedules collapse. Trades show up on the wrong day or not at all. Quality issues get buried under drywall before anyone catches them. A skilled project manager prevents all of that — and in the Sea to Sky Corridor, where logistics are already challenging, professional oversight isn't a luxury. It's a necessity.

Our project management team is led by Jeremy Lewis, who brings over 20 years of construction experience to every engagement. Jeremy joined Balmoral more than a decade ago and has managed builds ranging from compact mountain cabins to multi-million-dollar custom homes across the corridor. His approach is methodical and proactive — he identifies problems before they become expensive, and he communicates clearly so you're never left wondering what's happening on your site.

Working alongside Jeremy is Jonny Tweedie, who brings 17 years of finishing carpentry and site management experience. Originally from Northern Ireland, Jonny's background in high-end finishing work means he holds every trade to an exacting standard. He knows what quality looks like at every stage of construction because he's done the work himself. When Jonny flags an issue during a quality inspection, it's because he's spotted something that most people would miss entirely.

What Our Project Management Covers

Pre-Construction Planning — Before a single shovel hits the ground, we review your plans, assess constructability, identify potential risks, and develop a detailed project schedule. We work with your architect and engineers to ensure the design can be built efficiently and within your budget. This upfront planning is where the most expensive mistakes are prevented.

Budget Development & Tracking — We build a comprehensive project budget broken down by trade and phase, then track every dollar against it throughout construction. You'll receive regular budget reports showing where you stand, what's been committed, and where adjustments may be needed. No surprises at the end of the project — just transparent, ongoing financial visibility.

Schedule Management — We develop and maintain a detailed construction schedule that coordinates every trade, inspection, and material delivery. In the Sea to Sky, weather windows matter. Municipal inspection timelines vary between Whistler, Pemberton, and Squamish. We build contingency into every schedule because we know this corridor — and we adapt in real time when conditions change.

Trade Procurement & Oversight — Balmoral has long-standing relationships with subcontractors and tradespeople across the Sea to Sky Corridor. Whether you bring your own trades or need us to source them, we handle the coordination, scheduling, and quality oversight. Our relationships mean we can call on reliable crews even during the busiest construction seasons — something that's genuinely difficult in a market this tight.

Quality Control — We conduct regular site inspections at every critical phase of construction. From foundation pours to framing, envelope installation to finishing work, our team verifies that the work meets both the design specifications and BC Building Code requirements. Issues get documented and resolved before the next trade arrives on site.

Communication & Reporting — You'll know exactly where your project stands at all times. We provide structured progress reports, manage communication between you, your architect, and the trades, and make ourselves available when questions arise. Our goal is to give you confidence that your investment is being managed with the same care you'd give it yourself.

Why Standalone Project Management Makes Sense

There are several scenarios where dedicated project management — without full general contracting — is the right call. You may have a trusted framer or electrician you want on the job. You may be an owner-builder who needs professional scheduling and budget oversight. You may be an architect who wants a construction-side partner to manage the build on behalf of your client. Whatever the situation, our PM service gives you the structure and accountability of a professionally managed project without the overhead of a full GC contract.

Marc Harvey, Balmoral's owner, remains involved in every project management engagement. While Jeremy and Jonny handle day-to-day site operations, Marc provides strategic oversight and is always available to step in when decisions need to be made at the ownership level. This layered approach means you get both hands-on daily management and experienced executive oversight — the same model we use on our own full-build projects.
