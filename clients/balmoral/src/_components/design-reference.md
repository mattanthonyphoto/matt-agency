## Design System

### Colors (Construction brand — NOT photography)
- `#000000` — Primary text, headings
- `#333333` — Body text
- `#666666` — Secondary/meta text
- `#999999` — Muted text, placeholders
- `#F7F7F5` — Off-white page background
- `#FFFFFF` — White cards/sections
- `#EEEEEC` — Borders, dividers
- `#1A1A1A` — Dark sections (footer, CTA backgrounds)

NO gold, cedar, or warm accent colors. The photography carries the visual weight.

### Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 700 weight, tight letter-spacing (-.03em)
- Subheadings: 500 weight
- Body: 400 weight, #333, 1.75 line-height
- Meta/labels: 600 weight, 11px, uppercase, .15em letter-spacing, #999

### Layout Principles
- Max content width: 1200px
- Generous whitespace (80px+ vertical padding)
- Grid gallery (2-col, gap 12px) — NOT masonry
- Full-width hero images with minimal overlay
- Clean borders, no shadows

### What NOT to do (Matt's patterns to avoid)
- NO footer with dot separators (Based In · Email · Phone)
- NO small uppercase cedar/gold labels above headings
- NO masonry column galleries
- NO hero gradient overlays (just dark image or minimal scrim)
- NO gold/warm accent buttons
- NO serif fonts

### Navigation
- Fixed header, white bg on scroll, transparent on top
- Logo left, links right
- Simple dropdown on hover (not click)
- Mobile hamburger

### Footer
- Dark background (#1A1A1A)
- Footer mark logo centered at top
- Company description
- Three-column: Contact | Services | Navigation
- Copyright at bottom
- Social link (Instagram)

### Buttons
- Primary: Black bg, white text, uppercase, no border-radius
- Secondary: White bg, black border, black text
- Hover: invert

### CTA Sections
- Background image with dark overlay
- Centered text: heading + subtext + button
- "Ready to Discuss Your Project?" style (from Marc's current site)

### Project Page Structure
- Large hero image (no text overlay, just the image)
- Project title + location below image
- Metadata grid (architect, designer, etc.)
- Photo gallery in 2-column grid
- CTA section
- Footer
