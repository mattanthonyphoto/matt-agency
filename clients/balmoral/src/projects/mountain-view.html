An alpine home designed around the views that make Whistler unforgettable

Mountain View is a custom home in Whistler that does exactly what its name suggests — it puts the surrounding peaks and valleys at the centre of the living experience. From the earliest design conversations, the goal was clear: every principal room should connect its occupants to the landscape outside. The result is a home that feels open, grounded, and completely at home in its alpine setting.

The design takes full advantage of the site's orientation, with expansive glazing positioned to capture panoramic mountain views from the main living areas, the primary suite, and the upper deck. The window placement was carefully considered not just for the views themselves, but for how natural light moves through the home across seasons — from the long summer evenings to the soft, snow-filtered light of winter mornings.

Construction in Whistler always demands respect for the environment and the conditions it brings. Our team managed the build through variable weather, working with the terrain rather than against it to ensure the home sits naturally on its lot. The material choices reflect the alpine context — durable, weather-resistant exteriors paired with warm interior finishes that invite you to settle in after a day on the mountain.

Mountain View is the kind of project that reminds you why people build custom homes in the first place. It is not about square footage or checklists — it is about creating a home that belongs to its place, shaped by the land it sits on and the views it was built to frame.
