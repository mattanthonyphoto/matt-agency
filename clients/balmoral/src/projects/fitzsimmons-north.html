Balmoral's latest custom build taking shape in Whistler

Fitzsimmons North is Balmoral Construction's newest project in Whistler, currently rising from the ground in one of the valley's most sought-after locations. This custom home represents the next chapter in our commitment to building exceptional residences in the Sea to Sky Corridor — and watching it come together in real time is one of the most rewarding parts of what we do.

Building in Whistler during the winter months brings a unique set of challenges that our team knows well. Sub-zero temperatures, heavy snowfall, and shorter daylight hours all demand careful scheduling and experienced hands on site. Our crew works methodically through the conditions, protecting materials, managing moisture, and keeping the build on track without cutting corners. It is this kind of discipline that separates a reliable builder from the rest.

There is something special about a home in the early stages of construction. The framing goes up and suddenly the scale of the design becomes real — you can stand inside the future living room and already picture the mountain views that will fill the windows. The structure starts to tell its own story, and the craftsmanship that will define the finished home is already evident in every joint and connection.

We will be sharing progress on Fitzsimmons North as the build advances through the coming months. From foundation work to framing, envelope, and finishing, this project is a showcase of Balmoral's hands-on approach to custom home building in one of British Columbia's most demanding and beautiful environments.
