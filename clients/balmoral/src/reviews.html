Credentials

BC New Home Warranty

Registered residential builder under BC's mandatory new home warranty program. Every new home we build comes with 2-5-10 warranty coverage protecting your investment for up to a decade.

National Home Warranty

Additionally registered with National Home Warranty for comprehensive coverage. Dual registration means an extra layer of protection and accountability on every custom home build.

CHBA — Sea to Sky

Proud member of the Canadian Home Builders Association — Sea to Sky chapter. Active participation in the local building community keeps us current on best practices, regulations, and industry standards.
