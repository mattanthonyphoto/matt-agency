## Appendix B: AI Overview / Featured Snippet Strategy

Google's AI Overviews and featured snippets now dominate search results. To capture these:

### Format Blog Posts for Snippet Capture

1. **Use the question as an H2**, then answer in 40-60 words immediately below
2. **Include numbered lists** for process/steps content
3. **Use comparison tables** (timber frame vs. conventional, renovate vs. build new)
4. **Add FAQ sections** at the bottom of every blog post with FAQPage schema
5. **Include specific numbers** — Google AI Overviews love citing specific costs, timelines, and measurements

### Example FAQ Block (for every blog post)

```
## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does it cost to build a custom home on the Sunshine Coast?
Custom home construction on the Sunshine Coast starts at approximately $350/sq ft for standard builds. High-end custom homes with premium materials typically range from $500-$800+/sq ft. Marine access and remote island builds carry additional logistics costs.

### How long does it take to build a custom home on the Sunshine Coast?
A typical custom home build on the Sunshine Coast takes 12-18 months from breaking ground to move-in. Complex builds, remote island projects, or homes requiring marine access may take 18-24+ months.

### Do I need an architect to build a custom home?
Not always. Hanson Land & Sea offers both design-build services and architect collaboration. For complex or architecturally significant projects, we partner with firms like Battersby Howat and September Architecture to realize your vision.
```
